His Girl Friday; via YouTube

This is a standard YouTube embed, which can be found at the video's original site: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0AIC_UsPubs

The embed code is simply this:

    
<iframe width="800" height="600"
    src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/0AIC_UsPubs?rel=0"
    frameborder="0" allowfullscreen>
</iframe>

And yet your browser's Network Panel will show a barrage of files and scripts fired off by the YouTube video-playing program, including ad files. Compare it to the much more zen -- albeit clunkier -- experience when the website itself hosts the video file, sans third-party advertising scripts.
